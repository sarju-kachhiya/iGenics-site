Natural Ingredients Used In iGenics™
Bilberry

Bilberry extract is rich in anthocyanins, powerful antioxidants known for supporting eye health and visual clarity. These compounds help protect retinal cells from oxidative stress and environmental strain. Bilberry is widely used in vision support supplements to help maintain healthy night vision and overall ocular performance.

Ginkgo Biloba

Ginkgo Biloba is a plant extract traditionally used to support healthy circulation and cognitive function. In eye health formulas, it helps promote blood flow to ocular tissues, supporting the delivery of oxygen and nutrients needed for healthy vision and retinal function.

Turmeric & Bioperine

Turmeric contains curcumin, a well-known antioxidant that helps support a healthy inflammatory response in the body. When combined with Bioperine, a black pepper extract that enhances nutrient absorption, this blend supports overall eye comfort and antioxidant protection for long-term visual wellness.

Saffron

Saffron is a premium botanical ingredient studied for its antioxidant properties and role in supporting retinal health. It helps protect delicate eye structures from oxidative damage and supports visual sharpness as part of a balanced eye health supplement routine.

Zeaxanthin

Zeaxanthin is an essential carotenoid concentrated in the macula and widely recognized for supporting macular health and visual clarity. It acts as a natural blue light filter, helping protect the eyes from digital screen exposure while supporting sharp contrast and overall eye performance.
