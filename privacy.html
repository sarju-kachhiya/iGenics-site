Automatic Enrollment Notice: When purchasing a product, you may be subscribed to receive updates and relevant communications from our official mailing list.

2. Third-Party Interactions

We may disclose personal information when required by law or when necessary to protect the rights, safety, or property of our company, customers, or the public.

7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our services are intended for individuals aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If such data is discovered, it will be removed promptly.
